About

This data represents the ratio of rental vs owned houses in New York City. The building data table includes building-level information such as address, stories, and square footage at base about which HPD is required to report pursuant to Local Law 44 of 2012, and is part of the Housing Projects Receiving City Financial Assistance (Local Law 44) collection of data tables.

Data sourced from NYC Open Data
